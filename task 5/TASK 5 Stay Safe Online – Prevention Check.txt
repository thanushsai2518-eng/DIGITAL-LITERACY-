Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

Do not click on unknown links
Always check website URLs carefully
Never share OTP, PIN, or passwords
Use strong and unique passwords
Enable two-factor authentication (2FA)
Avoid using public Wi-Fi for transactions
Install antivirus and keep devices updated
Be careful with emails asking for personal details
Use trusted apps for UPI payments
Immediately report fraud at cybercrime.gov.in
Call 1930 helpline for financial fraud
